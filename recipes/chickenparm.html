Chicken Parmesan

Delicious and easy chicken parmesan receipe that is easy to make. This receipe is low calorie but still has tons of flavor.

Ingredients

  • Chicken brests
  • Butter
  • Grated Parmesan
  • Breadcrums
  • Lemon
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Shredded Mozarella
  • Green Beans

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
  2. Cut lemon into wedges. In a mixing bowl, combine breadcrums and parmesan.
  3. Pat chicken dry and flatten to an even thickness. Season with pepper.
  4. Brush chicken with melted butter, coat with breadcrum mixture.
  5. Place chicken on prepared baking sheet, spray evenly with cooking spray.
  6. Bake in oven until chicken reaches 165 degrees
  7. Cook green beans in skillet.
  8. Once chicken is cooked, top with marinara sauce and mozarella cheese. Bake in hot oven until cheese is melted.
