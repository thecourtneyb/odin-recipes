Chicken Gyros

Quick and easy 15 minute meal to make when on the go. The flavors of the gyro can be transffered to chicken with such ease. Opa!

Ingredients

  • Chimichurri Seasoning
  • Chicken Breasts, diced
  • Grape Tomatoes
  • Lemon Juice
  • Naan Flatbread
  • Shallot
  • Spring Green Mix
  • Tzatziki Dip

Steps

  1. Season chicken with a pinch of salt.
  2. Add diced chicken and seasoning blend to hot fry pan. Stir occasionally until chicken reaches an internal temp of 165 Degrees.
  3. Halve tomatoes.
  4. Peel and slice shallot thinly.
  5. Add shallot and tomatoes to hot pan and cook 2 minutes.
  6. Add 2tbsp water and lemon juice. Stir until tomatoes are soft.
  7. Warm flatbreads in hot dry pan until golden brown.
  8. Plate dish, filling flatbreads with filling and topping with the spring mix and tzatziki dip.
