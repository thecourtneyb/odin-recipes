BBQ Chicken Pizza

Delicious and savory BBQ Chicken Pizza made in under 30 minutes. Easy for busy nights on the go.

Ingredients

  • Naan bread
  • Chiken thighs
  • Sweet Baby Rays BBQ sauce
  • Pinapple chunks
  • Shallot
  • Green onions
  • Cheddar cheese

Cooking Steps

  1. Turn oven on to 400 degrees
  2. Mince shallot
  3. Cut green onions into thin slices
  4. Cut chiken into bit sized chunks
  5. Heat up medium fry pan on medium heat. Cook chicken until internal temp reaches 165 degrees. Coat chicken in BBQ sauce.
  6. while chiken cooks, prepare naan bread
  7. Sprinkle shallot and pinapple chunks on naan bread. Add cooked chicken when ready.
  8. Drizzle BBQ sauce on top of the pizzas.
  9. Sprinkle cheese on top of pizza.
  10. Bake in oven for 12 minutes, or until cheese is melted and naan is crispy.
  11. Enjoy!
